  • MANITOBA

Mile Ex restaurant Manitoba fittingly just feels like Canada and in a remarkably modern and fresh way that steers well away from tropes of maple syrup or lumberjacks. Chef Simon Mathys hearty meats and game and ample root vegetables; but without seasonal affective disorder, and in summer, the restaurant's backyard is one of the top terrasses in town. It's topped off with a neatly-curated natural wine list, too.

271 Saint-Zotique W O Montreal Ave, QC H2V 1A4, Canada. Ph: (514)-270-8000

  • IMPASTO

A premier purveyor of home-style Haitian food since 1984. This grocery store-cum-lunch counter serves a rich and flavourful pork griot, with fried breadfruit and rice and beans. Impasto offers a catering service at a location of your choice. It is also possible to reserve our restaurant for a private event. Group menus available. We offer many options; contact us for details.

48 Rue Dante Montreal, QC H2S 1J5, Canada. Ph: (514)-508-6508

  • DAMAS

Damas was Montreal's comeback restaurant of 2015. The story has been well-circulated by now: the original Damas was gutted by fire a year ago.
